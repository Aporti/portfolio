Innocuous AI

Designed with Adobe XD and Built with WebFlow

I designed and built the website of an artificial intelligence platform. I wanted to bring a modern design into an aging website. The original website looked old to me so I wanted to design a beautiful, effective website.

Modern Design

This website is the website of an artificial intelligence platform so it should be as modern as possible. I really wanted this website to stand out.

Effective Design

I wanted the user to click on links on top of the artificial intelligence platform to show them how it looks like. The customer can click on links next to "Remote Development" and it will change the underlying photo of the AI platform. This would increase conversion rates.

Unique Terminal

I created this design to fully show the customer how this AI platform can help them. The customer can click on the links next to "DevOps" and it will swipe right and show a different picture of the platform.
