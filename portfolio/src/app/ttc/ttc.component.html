I was responsible for designing a new medical laboratory website and bringing it to production with WordPress. I designed the website by trying to make it as unique as possible and by asking customers for their service. I also produced it in WordPress.

Call-to-Action

I really wanted to grab any user that comes onto our page and get their information. I think that trying to increase the conversion rate of our customers is extremely important because we are helping them with their health.

I wanted a layout that sells them the longer that they stay on the page

Well-Placed Q&A

Sales is all about meeting the customer's needs. I thought that if we placed a small Q&A on our page that we would be able to increase our conversion rate.

Step-by-Step Sales

I designed this website to fully sell the customer on the idea that it was an easy process.

Honest Selling

Being trustworthy to the client is an extremely important part of making sales. I thought that, by placing great photos of the technicians, we would be able to increase the conversion rate.
