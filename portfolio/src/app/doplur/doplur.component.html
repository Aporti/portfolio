Doplur, Inc.

Designed with Adobe XD

The CEO of this company wanted me to help them design a new social media podcast website. I really wanted to design a website that looked and felt easy to use. This was critically important because I wanted the users to upload as many podcasts as they desired.

Convenient Interface

With this interface, I thought that our users would be able to easily upload their own podcasts and post them on a timeline. They would be able to record their audio live or they could upload a pre-recorded podcast. They would be able to replay their old audio, listen to it and add comments on it.

Effective Search

I wanted to make it as easy as possible for the users to find what they are looking for throughout the whole site. They would have to type their search in the searchbar and the website would show their query.

Clean Sign Up Page

I wanted as clean of a signup page as possible, in order to entice users to sign up.
